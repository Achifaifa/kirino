Mob class. This defines and generates creeps moving in the dungeon on their own.

Libraries used:
os, random, dungeon

Functions:

__init__(self,dungeon):
Creates a creep on a random empty spot of the dungeon. The only condition is the tile not being rock (#).

move(self,dungeon,str direction,int distance):
Moves a creep on a dungeon a certain tiles (distance) on a certain direction (direction).
The direction must be a string (n, s, e, w).

randmove(self,dungeon,int dist):
Moves a creep in a dungeon a certain amount of tiles (dist) in a random direction.

trandmove(self,dungeon):
Moves a creep a random number of tiles in a random direction.
